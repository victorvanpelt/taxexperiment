  • Alophonica and Bellico have their headquarters in the country Olmeos.
  • However, next to Olmeos, both firms have the same business operations in a country named Hingland.
  • Although Olmeos has a statutory tax rate of 24%, Hingland's statutory tax rate is lower.
  • Olmeos allows firms to deduct 100% of their Research & Development (R&D) expenses from their profit before taxes as tax credits. That is, like all expenses, R&D expenses are first deducted from profit. Because of the tax credits they can be deducted for a second time to lower tax expenses.
